  • My parents are born in the city of the orange.
  • When called for, orange is my go-to artificial flavor of choice.
  • I have over ten orange themed decor pieces; most are gifts by friends.
  • My friends taught me that peeling and sharing the slices of an orange is widely considered an act of love as written in poems.
  • While walking my dogs, I noticed that orange is there for you at the end of the day, when the sun sets.
